PathFinder

Pathfinder is a web/mobile app that provides the best or quickest directions from one location to another location. While providing the best route to take, pathfinder also provides the distance between these two locations, how long the journey will take, giving a detailed description of the names of the roads to take and when to turn left or right, and also provides an image of a map to show in detail the route best for your needs. Pathfinder also gives you the option to add additional stops to your journey, showing the most efficient path to take, while also giving you the option to make your final destination point be in the vicinity of landmarks or services you might need, for example, if you’re traveling from home to college, but you want to get petrol on the way, pathfinder will map the quickest route to your college while also passing a petrol station.
